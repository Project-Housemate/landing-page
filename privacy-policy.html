Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2, 2025

Housemate (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email & Authentication: Collected when you sign up using Firebase Authentication.
  • Shared Content: Includes chore assignments, grocery list items, and quiet time preferences stored via Firestore.
  • Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to gather anonymous usage metrics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Authenticate and manage your account
  • Sync and share tasks among housemates
  • Improve app functionality through anonymous analytics

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on Google’s Firebase platform. Please review their Privacy Policy for more details.

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us at support@housemateapp.com.

5. Children’s Privacy

Housemate is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at support@housemateapp.com.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please email us at support@housemateapp.com.
