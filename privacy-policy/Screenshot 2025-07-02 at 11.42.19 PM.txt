9. DO UNITED STATES RESIDENTS HAVE SPECIFIC PRIVACY RIGHTS?

In Short: If you are a resident of California, Colorado, Connecticut, Delaware, Florida, Indiana, lowa, Kentucky, Maryland, Minnesota, Montana,
Nebraska, New Hampshire, New Jersey, Oregon, Rhode Island, Tennessee, Texas, Utah, or Virginia, you may have the right to request access to
and receive details about the personal information we maintain about you and how we have processed it, correct inaccuracies, get a copy of, or
delete your personal information. You may also have the right to withdraw your consent to our processing of your personal information. These rights
may be limited in some circumstances by applicable law. More information is provided below.
